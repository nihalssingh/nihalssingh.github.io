About me

I am pursuing my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UC Santa Barbara (UCSB).

I spend my research time developing full-stack probabilistic computing systems by doing the following:
• Take challenging optimization and machine learning problems,
• Find ways to map and implement them using a probabilistic computer,
• Translate these algorithms and applications down to scalable platforms combining emerging devices with CMOS.

Education: M.S., Electrical and Computer Engineering, UC Santa Barbara (UCSB),
B.E. (Hons.), Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Birla Institue of Technology and Science, Pilani, India
